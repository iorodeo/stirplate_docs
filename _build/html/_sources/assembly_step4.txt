Step 4: Assembly of the enclosure
================================================

Mounting the motor
----------------------

**Parts: 4 x motor standoffs, 4 x enclosure screws, 4 x motor mount screws, motor/motor mount assembly**


Place the four motor standoffs into the enclosure using four of the enclosure screws. Mount the motor onto the standoffs as shown in the image below using the four (longer) motor mount screws.

Assembly of arylic enclosure
---------------------------------------

**Parts: 5 x lasercut acrylic enclosure parts, 4 x enclosure standoffs, 8 x enclosure screws**

Place the last four enclosure standoffs into the corner of the enclosure base using four of the enclosure screws. Place the remaining enclosure sides and top onto the enclosure. Use four of the enclosure screws into each of the corner standoffs to hold everything in place.

.. figure:: step_4D.png
   :align:  center

.. figure:: step_4E.png
   :align:  center
